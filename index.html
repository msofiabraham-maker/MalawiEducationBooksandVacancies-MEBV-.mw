About MEBV

Malawi Education Books and Vacancies (MEBV) is a dedicated platform for providing quality educational resources to students and educators across Malawi.

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality educational materials, making learning resources affordable and accessible to every student in Malawi, regardless of socioeconomic background.

Our Vision

To become the leading educational resource platform in Malawi, empowering learners and educators with comprehensive study materials and career opportunities.
